The preprocessing folder uses ERA5 historical data (daily mean temperature for a selected 30 years time range) to calculate the temperature percentiles associated with the historical data. The percentiles are the ones prescribed in the Exposure Response Functions data provided by the authors from [Scovronick et al., 2024](https://journals.lww.com/environepidem/fulltext/2024/10000/temperature_mortality_associations_by_age_and.9.aspx). The final "map" of percentiles will serve to have a mapping between the percentiles of the pooled response functions and the daily temperatures of a given pixel.
